Reliance Mart offers a broad range of products, including groceries, apparel, footwear, toys, home improvement products, electronic goods, and farm implements and inputs. Reliance Retail has adopted a multi-format approach to serve its customers through diverse formats, including Reliance Mart, catering to various segments. Reliance Retail also operates Reliance Market, a wholesale cash and carry store chain aimed at supporting small businesses with a modern distribution system. Reliance Retail also operates Reliance Fresh, which focuses on grocery retail. Reliance Retail also has an online platform called JioMart, which offers a wide selection of products across multiple categories.
